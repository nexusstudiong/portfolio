Referral Program

NEXUS STUDIO REFERRAL PROGRAM

I truly appreciate everyone who helps my work reach new clients. To show my appreciation, I am willing to offer up to 30% of the total project value of every successful referral.

HOW DOES IT WORK

  1. Refer someone who needs a website, design, or other service I offer.
  2. If they hire me and the project is confirmed, your commission will be on the way.
  3. Once the upfront payment is made, you will receive your percentage from the upfront payment, and when the balance is paid, you will receive the rest of your commission.

COMMISSION PERCENTAGE

Your commission percentage is determined based on the value of the project.

  • 20% for any project whose value is less than ₦500,000
  • 30% for any project whose value is ₦500,000 or more

SALARIES / WAGES

If you refer someone that grants me a salary/wage (monthly or weekly pay) job, your commission is calculated based on the total monthly pay.

  • If the job pays ₦300,000 monthly, your commission is ₦60,000 per month
  • If the job pays ₦600,000 monthly, your commission is ₦180,000 per month
  • If the job pays ₦100,000 weekly, your commission is ₦80,000 per month
  • If the job pays ₦200,000 weekly, your commission is ₦240,000 per month
