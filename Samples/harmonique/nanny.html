HIRING PROCESS FOR NANNIES

At [Your Agency Name], we make the process of finding the right nanny as simple and seamless as possible. Here’s how it works:

1. Tell Us What You're Looking For – We start by listening to your specific needs, including your children's ages, care requirements, and schedule preferences. Whether you're looking for a live-in, live-out, or occasional nanny, we take note of your preferences and family dynamics.

2. We Find the Best Match – Based on your requirements, we carefully review our pool of qualified, vetted nannies to find the best match. We ensure they meet all of your needs, from experience level to certifications and personality.

3. Schedule an Interview – Once we’ve identified a great candidate, we schedule an interview for you to meet the nanny. This interview is an opportunity for you to ask questions, discuss expectations, and gauge the nanny's approach to childcare.

4. Trial Run & Meet-and-Greet – We recommend a trial run or meet-and-greet with the nanny to see if they are a good fit for your family. This allows you to observe their interaction with your children and make sure the connection feels right before committing.

5. Ongoing Support – After the trial, we follow up with you to ensure everything is going smoothly. If any adjustments need to be made, we’re here to support you every step of the way.
