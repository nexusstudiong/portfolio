OUR HIRING PROCESS

At Harmonique Services, we believe in a seamless and hassle-free process when it comes to hiring the right help for your family:

1. Tell Us What You Need: Let us know the type of service you're looking for, whether it's a nanny, PSW, or cleaning assistance. Be specific about your requirements, such as the hours needed, experience level, and any additional preferences.

2. We Find the Right Match: We use your preferences to find the most qualified candidates for your needs. Each candidate is thoroughly vetted, and we only present you with the best.

3. Interview & Trial Run: Once we find a match, we schedule an interview with your family to meet the candidate in person. This is also an opportunity for a trial run, so you can see if they’re a good fit.

4. Start the Service: After the interview and trial, if everything feels right, we’ll finalize the details and get started with the service.
